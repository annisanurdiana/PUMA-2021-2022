Agenda and Job Description

Chairperson

Building a spirit of cooperation and prioritizing togetherness among Computing faculty students. Optimizing the role of PUMA Computing in building the Computing faculty. Build a good relationship between puma of the Computing faculty with the academic community, PUSC, PUSU, and all PUMA at President University

Vice Chairperson

Facilitating the development of innovative, creative, and productivity of Computing faculty students in academic and non academic. Maintaining harmonious cooperation in puma Computing between BoD, Head of Division, all active member of Puma Computing, and alumni of Puma Computing. Continue and develop programs that have been implemented by the previous PUMA Computing organizer.

Secretary

Running the administrative function of the organization. Saving the organization on the records of incoming and outgoing letters. Making the MoM (Minutes of Meeting) in every PUMA meeting and sharing it to all members after the meeting. Responsible for every information of the Competizen, such as registration forms of ongoing seminars held by the Faculty or PUMA.

Treasure

Responsible for keeping the money in a safe and guranteed place. Ensure the use of funds all kept on a proper record. Prepare and provide financial reports monthly. Effective monitoring and reporting to everyone handling the money (e.g. class manager). Manager bank account properly.
