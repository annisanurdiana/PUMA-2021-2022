    Vision

    Make the President University Major Association of Computing interactive and responsive, so as to create organizations that productive, innovative, creative, active, and profesional.

    Mision

    ❖ Building a spirit of cooperation and prioritizing togetherness among Computing faculty students.
    ❖ Optimizing the role of PUMA Computing in building the Computing faculty
    ❖ Build a good relationship between puma of the Computing faculty with the academic community, PUSC, PUSU, and all PUMA at President University
    ❖ Facilitating the development of innovative, creative, and productivity of Computing faculty students in academic and non academic
    ❖ Maintaining harmonious cooperation in puma Computing between BoD, Head of Division, all active member of Puma Computing, and alumni of Puma Computing.
